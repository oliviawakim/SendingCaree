Sending Care to Others

Chick-fil-A’s Send Care to Others feature is a new ordering option that allows guests to customize a meal for someone special.
Complete with exclusive packaging, add-ons, and a personalized note, care packages can be ordered through the app and delivered to the recipient’s doorstep with Care.

Project Details

Care Packages

Send a customizable care package to loved ones by choosing from a variety of gift box sleeves for different occasions, selecting from exclusive add-ons,and including a personalized note.

Moments That Matter

Chick-fil-A believes in being more than just a restaurant - they strive to be a part of moments that matter, whether it’s a birthday celebration or just enjoying a delicious meal with friends.

Digital Gift Giving

For locations without delivery, digital gift cards offer a unique and thoughtful sending experience that will make friends and family feel extra special.

Just a Tap Away

Chick-fil-A has made it quicker and easier than ever to send others a delicious gift conveniently through the mobile app.
